Project Bharat One



In an ever-changing world where unforeseen emergencies can strike at any moment, So being prepared and connected all the time is all we need. Meet Bharat One an all-encompassing public safety and emergency assistance app designed to be your steadfast companion in times of crisis. Whether facing a natural disaster, a sudden accident, or any unforeseen event, Bharat One stands ready to offer you vital support and timely information.


With its user-centric approach, Bharat One brings an array of essential features to your fingertips:


  • Immediate Emergency Reporting: Just a few taps are all it takes to report an emergency, notifying authorities and responders swiftly.

  • Real-time Incident Updates : Stay informed about the latest incidents in your vicinity, enabling you to make informed decisions.

  • Vital Safety Information : Access critical safety details such as evacuation routes and nearby shelter locations, ensuring you and your loved ones can navigate emergencies effectively.

  • Connected Communication : Engage with emergency responders and fellow users in your area, fostering a sense of community and shared safety efforts.

  • Simplified Processes : Streamline essential tasks like insurance claims and lost property reporting, minimizing post-crisis stress.

Available in multiple languages and functional offline, Bharat One guarantees your safety, regardless of your location. Don't wait any longer – empower yourself and safeguard your loved ones by downloading Bharat One today. Embrace a new level of preparedness and security that Bharat One brings to your fingertips.--CURRENTLY IN PROTOTYPE STAGE


Some Highlighted Features :


» A panic button that users can press to instantly send an emergency alert to their contacts and emergency services. The panic button could also be configured to automatically share the user's location along with details to their contacts.

» A safety chat feature that allows users to connect with other users in the area who are also using Bharat One. This feature could be used to coordinate safety efforts during an emergency, or simply to connect with other people.

» A community forum where users can share safety tips and information. This forum couldbe a valuable resource for users who are looking for information about how to stay safe in their community.


Here are some other useful points to consider :


» Bharat One could be integrated with other apps and services, such as social media & maps, This would allow users to share information with their friends and family.
» Bharat One could be used to promote public safety awareness. The app could include educational content about safety topics, such as how to prevent crime, how to stay safe during natural disasters, and how to provide first aid etc.
» Bharat One could be used to collect data about public safety incidents. This data could be used to improve public safety planning and response.


Details Collected From the user :


» Personal Information :
Name : Full name of the user.
Age : Age of the user to ensure age-appropriate assistance.
Gender : Optional, to personalize the app experience if the user prefers


» Contact Information :
Email Address : To send important updates, alerts, and notifications.
Phone Number : Primary contact number for emergency communications.
Home Address : To help emergency services locate the user accurately.


» Languages Spoken :
Provide an option to select the languages the user is proficient in, to enable multilingual support during emergencies.


» Virtual Emergency :
Current Location : To customize emergency alerts based on the user's geographical location.
Household Members : Number of people in the user's household, including pets, if any.
Mobility Level : Assess whether the user has any mobility challenges to provide suitable evacuation options.
Medical Conditions : Any pre-existing medical conditions that emergency responders should be aware of.
Emergency Kit Status : Determine if the user has an emergency kit prepared and provide guidance if not.
Escape Routes : Identify the user's primary and alternative escape routes from their home or workplace.


» Emergency Contacts :
Allowing users to add emergency contacts such as family members, friends, or neighbors. Providimg an option to notify these contacts in case of emergencies.


» Verification and Security :
Implement verification methods (e.g., email or phone verification) to ensure the accuracy of user information.
